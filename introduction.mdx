## Welcome to Fossapay

Fossapay is the banking infrastructure platform that enables neobanks and fintechs across Africa to launch and scale financial products quickly and compliantly.

### What is Fossapay?

Fossapay provides a complete banking infrastructure through a single API, allowing you to:

- **Create Fiat Wallets** - Create and assign fiat wallets on demand for your customers
- **Manage Payments** - Accept collections and send payouts seamlessly
- **Instant Settlements** - Settle transactions in real-time in fiat or stablecoins
- **Build Wallets** - Create customer wallets with multi-currency support
- **Stay Compliant** - KYC-ready, PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure

## Core Features

### Collections API
Accept payments through fiat wallets with instant webhook notifications on every transaction.

### Payouts API
Send funds to any Nigerian bank account in seconds. Support for single and bulk disbursements with automated processing.

### Wallet Infrastructure
Create customer wallets, hold balances in NGN and stablecoins, and manage ledgers with ease.

### Compliance & Security
KYC-ready, PCI-DSS compliant, and built for high availability with 99.9% uptime guarantee.
